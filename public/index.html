Kansas Conspiracy

You're probably wondering how Kansas' average attendance is greater than their arena capacity. This is because Allen Fieldhouse utilizes bench seating, so they're able to cram more people per row than their alloted capacity. Someone should alert the Big 12 refs.

Elite Eight

The 1999-2000 season was one for the history books. The Cyclones finished 14–2 in conference play to earn the outright Big 12 Conference regular season championship - the first since 1945 and sixth ever. The Cyclones then defeated Oklahoma 70–58 in the Big 12 Tournament finals to win the Championship, the second conference tournament title in ISU men's basketball history. The #2 seed Cyclones advanced to the Elite Eight of the 2000 NCAA Tournament after wins over #15 Central Connecticut State, #7 Auburn, and #6 UCLA, but ultimately fell to Michigan State, the eventual NCAA Champion. This was the Cyclones' deepest NCAA Tournament run in the modern era.

Slow & Steady

This chart presents two key points: the slow and steady decline of the average teams' tempo, and the sluggish pace of the Greg McDermott era (2007-2010). This tempo reduction is what has prompted the NCAA to make rule changes encouraging more freedom of movement and fewer charging fouls.
